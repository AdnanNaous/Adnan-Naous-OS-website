# Private and excluded bookmark groups

Date reviewed: 2026-07-21

## Excluded by default

- University systems: student-information, learning-management, institutional email, and account portals. These are classified `University Private` and must remain outside the public site.
- Account-specific applications: web messaging, team workspaces, personal bookmark managers, and authenticated dashboards. These are classified `Account-Specific` or `Personal Only`.
- Private productivity state: personal workspaces, saved dashboards, and URLs containing account or team identifiers.
- Download and media utilities: downloader folders and similar links are archive candidates rather than public portfolio resources.
- Uncurated entertainment links: streaming and media destinations are excluded unless a future public-resource purpose is approved.
- URL parameters and fragments: tracking, session-like, team, or account-specific values are not copied into reports or public records.

## Counts

- Clearly private/excluded first-pass records: 15 total (1 Personal Only, 3 University Private, 5 Account-Specific, and 6 Archive Candidate).
- Additional records requiring confirmation: 123. These remain excluded until individually reviewed.
- Exact duplicate URLs: 0. Repeated domains are treated as separate source records unless they resolve to the same normalized resource.

## Retention rule

Nothing is deleted from the external export. Private and excluded entries remain available only in that source. The repository contains a sanitized hashed ledger for reconciliation and a separate candidate-public shortlist.
